# Team Meeting Agenda - Using Six Thinking Hats

## Meeting: New Feature Launch Decision

**Duration**: 90 minutes
**Participants**: Product Team, Engineering, Marketing, Design
**Facilitator**: Sarah (Blue Hat)

---

## Agenda

### Blue Hat (5 min) - Opening
**Facilitator**: Sarah

- **Objective**: Decide whether to launch Feature X in Q1
- **Sequence**: White → Red → Green → Yellow → Black → Green → Red → Blue
- **Ground Rules**:
  - One hat at a time for everyone
  - Respect the hat rules (e.g., no criticism in Green Hat)
  - Note thoughts for each hat separately
  - I'll signal when to switch hats

---

### White Hat (10 min) - Facts & Information
**Focus**: What do we know for certain?

**Data to Review**:
- Q1 timeline: 8 weeks remaining
- Development status: 60% complete
- Budget remaining: $50,000
- Competitor: Company Y launching similar feature in March
- Customer requests: 156 feature requests for this functionality
- Technical debt: Would add 15% to codebase complexity

**Questions to Answer**:
- What are our hard deadlines?
- What resources are available?
- What's the market situation?
- What do customers want?
- What are the constraints?

**Capture**:
- [ ] List all known facts
- [ ] Identify information gaps
- [ ] Note what we need to research

---

### Red Hat (5 min) - Feelings & Intuition
**Focus**: gut reactions, no justification required

**Prompt**: "How do you feel about launching in Q1?"

**Capture**:
- [ ] Individual feelings (anonymous if desired)
- [ ] Team concerns or excitement
- [ ] Intuition about timing

**Sample**:
- "I feel anxious about the timeline"
- "My gut says this is the right time despite the challenges"
- "I'm excited but cautious"

---

### Green Hat (15 min) - Creative Alternatives
**Focus**: How could we make this work? What are all the possibilities?

**Prompts**:
- What if we launched a minimal version?
- What if we postponed and added more features?
- What if we changed the approach entirely?
- What other options exist?

**Capture**:
- [ ] Generate as many ideas as possible
- [ ] No criticism or evaluation yet
- [ ] Build on each other's ideas

**Sample Ideas**:
- Launch MVP version with 20% of features
- Partner with beta customers for extended testing
- Staged rollout by customer segment
- Delay to Q2 but preempt competitor with teaser campaign
- Pivot to different feature set
- Outsource development to accelerate timeline

---

### Yellow Hat (10 min) - Benefits & Value
**Focus**: What's good about launching in Q1?

**Prompts**:
- What are the benefits of Q1 launch?
- Why would this work?
- What value would we create?

**Capture**:
- [ ] List all potential benefits
- [ ] Identify positive outcomes
- [ ] Note advantages of Q1 timing

**Sample**:
- Beat competitor to market
- Meet customer demand
- Q1 revenue impact
- First-mover advantage
- Team momentum and morale
- Learning for future features

---

### Black Hat (15 min) - Caution & Risks
**Focus**: What could go wrong? What are the risks?

**Prompts**:
- What are the risks of Q1 launch?
- What might not work?
- What problems could arise?

**Capture**:
- [ ] List all potential problems
- [ ] Identify risks
- [ ] Note concerns

**Sample**:
- Timeline overrun risk
- Quality issues from rushing
- Team burnout
- Technical debt accumulation
- Incomplete testing
- Negative customer experience
- Revenue impact of bug fixes

---

### Green Hat (10 min) - Solutions to Black Hat Concerns
**Focus**: How can we address the risks identified?

**Prompts**:
- For each Black Hat concern, what's the solution?
- How can we mitigate risks?

**Capture**:
- [ ] Pair solutions with risks
- [ ] Generate creative mitigations

**Sample**:
- Timeline risk: Cut scope to 80%, hire contractor
- Quality risk: Extend QA period, beta testing
- Team burnout: Bring in additional resources, adjust deadlines
- Technical debt: Allocate cleanup time after launch

---

### Red Hat (5 min) - Feelings After Analysis
**Focus**: How do you feel now after considering all perspectives?

**Prompt**: "Now that we've looked at all sides, what's your gut feeling?"

**Capture**:
- [ ] Have feelings changed?
- [ ] What's your intuition now?

---

### Blue Hat (10 min) - Decision & Next Steps
**Focus**: What's our decision and what's next?

**Decision**:
- [ ] Launch MVP in Q1 (reduced scope)
- [ ] Decision rationale: Benefits outweigh risks with mitigation plan

**Action Items**:
1. **Scope**: Define MVP features (Product Lead) - by Friday
2. **Resources**: Approve contractor budget (Manager) - by Friday
3. **Timeline**: Create detailed project plan (Engineering Lead) - by Monday
4. **Quality**: Design testing approach (QA Lead) - by Monday
5. **Communication**: Prepare customer announcement (Marketing) - draft by Friday
6. **Follow-up**: Weekly Blue Hat check-ins - every Thursday

**Next Blue Hat Session**: Thursday, 2:00 PM - Review progress and adjust
